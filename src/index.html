What is Ozonetherapy ?

Lack of oxygen leads to fatigue, the occurrence of diseases, disorders of the immune system, accelerated aging. Ozone (O3 ) – it is a gas with a characteristic odor. It is a type of oxygen, its molecule consists of 3 atoms, unlike the oxygen that we breathe (О2 ), whose molecule contains 2 atoms. The triatomic molecule of ozone provides its great activity in joining living cells and a high ability to oxidize. Ozone introduced into the body contributes to a significant saturation of blood and tissues with oxygen. Being an active form of oxygen, it fights with many toxins, bacteria, viruses, destroying them together with sick and defective cells, protects us from diseases.
